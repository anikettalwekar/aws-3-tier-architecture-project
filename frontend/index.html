About Our Club - Dream Cricket Club

Dream Cricket Club, established in 2024, is the vision of Aniket, a passionate cricketer and true lover of the game. Built on dedication, teamwork, and the spirit of sportsmanship, our club is a home for players who dream big and play with heart.

From local matches to competitive tournaments, we aim to nurture raw talent, promote fitness, and create a strong community of cricket enthusiasts.
